About Me


Hi, welcome to my homepage! I am a senior student at CUHK-Shenzhen, majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. During my undergraduate studies, I have been fortunate to be advised by Prof. Benyou Wang and to collaborate closely with Prof. Honghai Yu’s team at Nanjing University. I am also a member of FinAI, led by Jimin Huang. Prior to that, I had the opportunity to work with Prof. Jianfeng Mao.

Previously, my research was focused on:
(1) Data Mining: Spatial-Temporal Modeling, Time Series, Graph Learning,
(2) Large Language Model: Multi-modal Reasoning & Alignment, Agent,
(3) Trustworthy AI: Understanding Multi-modal or Unstructured Data,

Additionally, I am exploring the integration of LLMs with graph learning to address a broader range of real-world challenges: (1) how to leverage LLMs to develop a graph foundation model, and (2) how to design large-scale graph-based agents to simulate social phenomena within networks and financial domains.

Currently, I am seeking a PhD or RA position for 2025 Fall. If you are interested in my research or have any questions, please feel free to contact me.
